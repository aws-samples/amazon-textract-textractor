Document Entities
==================

:class:`Document` objects contain various entities within them. Textract document analysis APIs recognize 6 document entities namely: WORD, LINE, KEY_VALUE_SET 
, SELECTION_ELEMENT, TABLE, CELL 

These are structures that occur in most documents and the package provides classes to programmatically store and access the information 
produced by Textract for these entities.
